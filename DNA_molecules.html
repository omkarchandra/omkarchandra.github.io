The genome in most organisms is made up of RNA or DNA. This ability is ingrained since the formation of RNA/DNA, in fact, DNA underwent evolution to become longer (3 billion bp in human genome) and stronger (proof-reading enzymes for error corrections) manifesting itself into a more organized cognitive agents in the form of single cells and multicellular life forms.

So what is fighting the entropy? It is the ability to be organized, not to dissolve into dark abyss of the universe. This begs the question, can we design other molecule like DNA that can give rise to cognitive agents? The answer is yes, using the actual turing tapes (computers).
